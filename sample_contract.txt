MASTER SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This Master Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024 ("Effective Date") 
by and between Acme Software Corp., a Delaware corporation ("Vendor"), and BetaCo Inc., 
a California corporation ("Customer").

1. SUBSCRIPTION TERM
The initial subscription term shall commence on February 1, 2024 and continue through January 31, 2025 
("Initial Term"). This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods 
("Renewal Terms") unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 60 days 
prior to the end of the then-current term.

2. FEES AND PAYMENT

2.1 Subscription Fees
Customer shall pay Vendor a total annual fee of $120,000.00 USD for the services described herein. 
Fees shall be invoiced monthly at $10,000.00 per month.

2.2 Payment Terms
All invoices are due and payable within thirty (30) days of the invoice date (Net 30). 
Payment shall be made via ACH transfer or wire transfer to the account specified by Vendor.

2.3 Late Payment
Any amounts not paid within the payment terms shall bear interest at a rate of 1.5% per month 
(or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is lower), calculated from the date 
payment was due until the date of actual payment. A grace period of 5 days applies before 
interest begins to accrue.

3. USAGE TIERS

The subscription includes access based on the following seat-based pricing model:

Tier 1 (Starter):     1-10 seats     — $500/seat/month
Tier 2 (Growth):      11-50 seats    — $450/seat/month  
Tier 3 (Scale):       51-200 seats   — $400/seat/month
Tier 4 (Enterprise):  200+ seats     — Custom pricing, contact sales

In addition to seat fees, API usage above 1,000,000 calls per month shall be billed at 
$0.002 per additional API call.

4. RENEWAL AND CANCELLATION

The Agreement automatically renews annually unless cancelled. Customer must provide 
written notice of cancellation no later than sixty (60) days before the end of the 
current subscription period. Cancellations received after this deadline will take effect 
at the end of the following subscription period.

5. SPECIAL TERMS

5.1 Volume Discount: Customer is entitled to a 15% discount on list pricing for the first 
renewal term, provided Customer has paid all invoices on time during the Initial Term.

5.2 Price Lock: Vendor agrees not to increase subscription pricing by more than 7% annually 
for the first three (3) Renewal Terms.

5.3 Implementation: One-time implementation fee of $5,000 is due upon execution of this Agreement.
